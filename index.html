Who We Are

A New Standard in Financial Excellence

EM & Associate CPA(K) is a registered firm of Certified Public Accountants based in Kenya, committed to providing high-integrity, results-driven financial and advisory services to a diverse portfolio of clients.

From small and medium enterprises to non-governmental organizations and corporates, we bring clarity, compliance, and confidence to every engagement. Our work is rooted in the highest professional standards as defined by ICPAK and international best practices.

We believe that sound financial governance is not a luxury — it is the foundation upon which sustainable organizations are built.
